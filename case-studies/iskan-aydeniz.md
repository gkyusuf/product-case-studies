# İskan Aydeniz — career guidance center website

## Context

Freelance project for İskan Aydeniz, a career guidance and counseling center in Turkey. The client needed a professional web presence to present services, publish content, and provide a contact channel for prospective students and parents.

## Problem

The center had no dedicated website. Prospective clients could not easily review services, success metrics, or recent content before reaching out. The client required a maintainable site that reflected a professional education brand without ongoing developer dependency for basic updates.

## Approach

End-to-end delivery of a content-focused marketing website:

- Homepage with service overview and key metrics (placement statistics, student outcomes)
- Blog section for guidance and career-related articles
- Contact and inquiry flow
- Responsive layout for mobile and desktop

The site is deployed and maintained as a standalone project. Source code is not public per client agreement.

### Tech stack and delivery

- HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript
- Responsive layout for mobile and desktop
- Production deployment and hosting management

## Outcome

- Live at [iskanaydeniz.com](https://iskanaydeniz.com)
- Delivered as a complete freelance engagement (design through deployment)
- Supports the center's student acquisition and content publishing workflow
